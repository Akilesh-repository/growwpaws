Where We Started

Built Out of Necessity.

When our own working breeds struggled with fatigue and joint stiffness, we realized the commercial pet food industry was failing them. So, we changed it.

2020

The Realization

We were training heavily for agility competitions, but our dogs were struggling to maintain muscle mass. Commercial diets were full of cheap fillers. We needed pure protein isolates and bioavailable nutrients for true muscle recovery.

2022

Partnering with Experts

We collaborated with top veterinary nutritionists. We focused entirely on isolating key amino acids for strength and developing a rich fish oil blend to proactively support and lubricate joints during high-impact activities.

Present Day

A Movement in Nutrition

Today, PawCare is trusted by thousands of owners who view their dogs as athletes. We deliver precision-calculated, high-yield meals directly to your door, perfectly timed with your recurring schedule.

Uncompromising Quality

Crafted for Muscle. Built for Longevity.

Just like a human athlete tracking macros, an active dog requires specific nutrient profiles to perform optimally and recover safely without joint pain.

Pure Protein Isolates

We remove unnecessary carbs and fillers. Our recipes utilize rapidly absorbing proteins that repair torn muscle fibers instantly after heavy running or training.

High-Yield Fish Oils

Active joints take a beating. Our concentrated Omega-3 fish oil blends deeply lubricate joints, significantly reducing wear and tear and eliminating morning stiffness.

Zero Artificial Fillers

We never use cheap binders. Every gram in our formulas serves a biological purpose, ensuring maximum energy conversion and gut health.
