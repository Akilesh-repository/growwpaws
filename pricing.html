How are prices calculated?

Prices scale based on your dog's specific macro profile. A 90lb working German Shepherd will require more daily protein and volume than a 30lb spaniel.

Can I switch plans later?

Absolutely. If your dog enters an off-season or begins heavy agility training, you can adjust their macro tier instantly from your owner dashboard.

Is shipping included?

Yes. All subscription tiers include fast, nationwide shipping. Your customized box arrives on the exact same day every single month.

What if my dog is picky?

Our pure protein isolates are highly palatable, but if they refuse to eat it, our 30-day guarantee protects your purchase completely.
